Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning (HVAC)

We install equipment from all major big brands in the country. We use high quality insulated panels to create temperature controlled hygienic environments throughout Kenya, from cold ooms & hygienic food processing environments, through to chilled distribution warehouses & walk-in freezers, Manufacturing sites, Hotels & Resturants, Night Clubs & Pubs, Airports & Rail Stations, Hospitals and Supermarkets.

Plumbing & Kitchen Equipment

We do plumbing works & consistent maintenance of kitchen hood exhaust systems in resturants or facilities. Removing and cleaning grease & flamable residue from the interior hood surface, filters, ductworks & exhaust fans. We also supply kitchen equipment and do repairs for resturants, hotels, motels and learning institutions.
