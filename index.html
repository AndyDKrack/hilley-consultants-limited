Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning

We install equipment from all major big brands in the country. We use high quality insulated panels to create temperature controlled hygienic environments throughout Kenya, from cold ooms & hygienic food processing environments, through to chilled distribution warehouses & walk-in freezers, Manufacturing sites, Hotels & Resturants, Night Clubs & Pubs, Airports & Rail Stations, Hospitals and Supermarkets.

Building, Construction & Renovation Works

Buildings, roads, bridges - we build just about anything in the country. With a dedicated team that is very creative and ready to take on tasks that have never been attempted before. No matter the project, we apply our collective experience & Ingenuity to everything we do.
We contibute to a better society through our building construction works and impact on the effect of care & sound environment. We ensure clean structural connectivity in this modern life of industrial growth.

Plumbing & Kitchen Equipment

We do plumbing works & consistent maintenance of kitchen hood exhaust systems in resturants or facilities. Removing and cleaning grease & flamable residue from the interior hood surface, filters, ductworks & exhaust fans.
We also supply kitchen equipment and do repairs for resturants, hotels, motels and learning institutions.
